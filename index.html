Hi There! I'm Michael Pham. I'm a developer that loves

A little about me.

I've made my home base in Sacramento, CA with my wife and cat. My journey into tech started with a degree in Information Technology. As I started to learn how to develop code and design, I was fascinated with how art & tech can create such an impact. I was hooked and wanted to learn how to build my own site to fit my own specific needs.

That curiosity then opened a door that could never be shut. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I've made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.
